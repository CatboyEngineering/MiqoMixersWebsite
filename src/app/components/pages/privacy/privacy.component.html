Privacy Policy

Information Collected

We ("MiqoMixers") collect information from you for the purposes of providing service to your account. This information is required, and includes an email address, password, and connected Final Fantasy: XIV character. Some of this information, such as your Final Fantasy: XIV character name, may be shown to site visitors in accordance with our terms of use.

How it is Collected

MiqoMixers only collects information that you grant to us:

  • On the website, during registration, venue posting, and profile editing

In addition, the MiqoMixers website uses third-party tools to evaluate your request and ensure the safety of the service from cyber threats and attacks. Your data in those cases will be governed by those tools' respective privacy policies:

  • Google ReCaptcha
  • Google Fonts
  • Font Awesome
  • MongoDB Atlas

How We Use Your Information

Your data is collected in order to be processed for the sole purpose of providing the MiqoMixers service. Some data collected automatically, such as through Google ReCaptcha, is used to calculate a browser risk score during registration and log-in. This data is processed under Google ReCaptcha's respective policies, and MiqoMixers is only made aware of the final score result.

Information you explicitly provide to us, such as log-in information, is processed and stored in a secure database according to modern security best practices. Venue posts, profile information, and other user-generated data is processed in an encrypted fashion and used only to provide functioning service.

Sharing of Information

We do not share your data with any third-parties apart from the parties listed in the previous sections for the reasons given, except where required by law. We will never share your data for marketing or promotional purposes.

Certain pieces of your data, such as your public Final Fantasy: XIV character name, may be shared with other users on the service only alongside public Venue posts made by you.

Your Rights

Under this privacy policy, you have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. This process can be done by the user after authenticating on the MiqoMixers website. We do not support contacting the site admin to assist with data deletion at this time. We also do not provide a means for users to download a copy of their data.
